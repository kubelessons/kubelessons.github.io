# Welcome to Kubelessons!

This website is designed to help you learn and practice Kubernetes in a hands-on way using open-source studying materials. It is in the process of being built.

## Why Kubelessons?
- **Free video lessons**: Listen to selected [Lessons](/lessons/)
- **Hands-on Learning**: Set up your own Kubernetes cluster with [MiniKube](/minikube/).
- **Practice Exams**: Test your knowledge with our [Mock Exams](/mock-exams/).
